# $ave+ Automations Guide

## Overview

Automations in $ave+ help you save money effortlessly by automating recurring transfers and round-ups. Set your rules once, and let the system work for you.

---

## Types of Automations

### 1. Scheduled Deposits

Automatically transfer a fixed amount to your savings on a recurring schedule.

**Features:**
- Choose from weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly frequency
- Set custom start dates
- Pause/resume anytime
- Edit amounts and schedules on the fly

**How to Create:**
1. Navigate to `/automations`
2. Click "New Automation"
3. Enter automation name (e.g., "Weekly Savings")
4. Set the amount you want to save
5. Choose frequency (weekly/bi-weekly/monthly)
6. Select start date
7. Add optional notes
8. Click "Create Automation"

### 2. Round-ups

Save spare change automatically by rounding up transactions to the nearest dollar.

**How it Works:**
- Purchase for $4.30 → Save $0.70
- Purchase for $12.99 → Save $0.01
- All round-ups are processed daily
- Automatically transferred to your default savings goal

**Enable Round-ups:**
1. Go to Automations page
2. Toggle "Enable Round-ups" switch
3. View your recent savings in the card

### 3. Safety Rules

Protect your finances with automatic safeguards.

**Low Balance Protection:**
- Set a minimum balance threshold (default: $200)
- All scheduled transfers pause automatically when balance drops below threshold
- Resumes when balance recovers
- Prevents overdraft and low balance fees

**Edit Safety Rule:**
1. Click "Edit" on the Safety Rules card
2. Enter new minimum balance (minimum $50)
3. Click the checkmark to save

## Managing Automations

### View All Automations
- Navigate to `/automations` to see all your scheduled deposits
- Active automations show a green "Active" badge
- Paused automations show a gray "Paused" badge

### Edit an Automation
1. Click the edit icon (pencil) on any automation card
2. Update name, amount, frequency, or start date
3. Click "Update Automation"

### Pause an Automation
1. Click the power icon on any automation card
2. Automation status changes to "Paused"
3. No transfers will occur while paused
4. Click power icon again to resume

### Delete an Automation
1. Click the trash icon on any automation card
2. Confirm deletion in the dialog
3. Automation is permanently removed

## Best Practices

### Getting Started
1. **Start Small**: Begin with a comfortable amount (e.g., $25/week)
2. **Enable Round-ups**: Set it and forget it
3. **Set Safety Rules**: Protect your account balance
4. **Review Monthly**: Adjust amounts as your income changes

### Maximizing Savings
- **Pay Yourself First**: Schedule transfers right after payday
- **Increase Gradually**: Bump up amounts by $10-25 each quarter
- **Combine Methods**: Use both scheduled deposits and round-ups
- **Align with Goals**: Schedule more when saving for big goals

### Safety Tips
- **Monitor Balances**: Check your linked account regularly
- **Set Realistic Amounts**: Don't over-commit
- **Use Safety Rules**: Always set a minimum balance threshold
- **Pause When Needed**: Life happens - pause automations during tight months

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: When do scheduled transfers happen?**
A: Transfers process early morning on the scheduled date.

**Q: Can I have multiple automations?**
A: Yes! Create as many as you need for different goals or schedules.

**Q: What happens if I miss a payment?**
A: If a transfer fails (insufficient funds), you'll receive a notification. The system will attempt the transfer on the next scheduled date.

**Q: Can I schedule automations to specific goals?**
A: Currently, automations transfer to your primary savings. Goal-specific automations are coming soon!

**Q: Do round-ups work with all purchases?**
A: Round-ups process on debit card transactions from your linked checking account.

**Q: How do I know my money is safe?**
A: $ave+ uses bank-level encryption and never stores your banking credentials. All transfers are processed through secure, regulated channels.

**Q: Can I export my automation history?**
A: Yes, navigate to Settings → Export Data to download your complete automation history.

## Troubleshooting

**Automation didn't run on schedule:**
1. Check that automation is Active (not Paused)
2. Verify your linked account has sufficient balance
3. Ensure safety rules didn't pause transfers
4. Check for any email notifications about failed transfers

**Round-ups not working:**
1. Verify round-ups toggle is ON
2. Confirm your checking account is linked
3. Allow 24-48 hours for initial setup
4. Contact support if issue persists

**Can't create new automation:**
1. Check you haven't reached account limits
2. Verify all required fields are filled
3. Ensure start date is today or future
4. Try refreshing the page
