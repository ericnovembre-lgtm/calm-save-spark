## LifeSim: Financial Life Simulator

### Overview
LifeSim is an interactive game that simulates your financial life from early career through retirement. Make decisions about education, career, housing, relationships, and investments to see how they impact your long-term financial health.

### Getting Started

1. **Access LifeSim**
   - Navigate to Features Hub
   - Click on "LifeSim" card
   - Click "Start New Simulation"

2. **Configure Your Game**
   - **Session Name**: Give your game a memorable name
   - **Starting Age**: Choose between 22-30 years old
   - **Target Retirement Age**: Set your goal between 55-70

3. **Understanding the Dashboard**
   - **Annual Income**: Your yearly earnings
   - **Annual Expenses**: Your yearly spending
   - **Savings Rate**: Percentage of income saved
   - **Total Debt**: Outstanding loans and credit

### Making Decisions

Each turn, you'll face a life decision with 3 choices:
- **Conservative Choice**: Lower risk, smaller impact
- **Moderate Choice**: Balanced risk and reward

## Investment Manager

### Overview
The Investment Manager helps you define your investment strategy through an automated mandate system. Set your risk tolerance, rebalancing rules, and tax optimization preferences.

### Creating Your Mandate

1. **Navigate to Investment Manager**
   - Go to Features Hub â†’ Investment Manager

2. **Set Risk Tolerance**
   - **Conservative**: 40% stocks, 60% bonds
   - **Moderate**: 60% stocks, 40% bonds
   - **Aggressive**: 80% stocks, 20% bonds

3. **Configure Rebalancing**
   - **Threshold**: How far from target before rebalancing (1-10%)
   - **Automatic**: Enable auto-rebalancing when threshold exceeded
   - Lower thresholds = more frequent rebalancing
   - Higher thresholds = fewer trades, lower costs

4. **Tax-Loss Harvesting**
   - **Enable TLH**: Automatically harvest losses for tax benefits
   - **Minimum Amount**: Set minimum loss size to harvest ($1000-$10000)
   - Helps reduce tax liability on capital gains

### Understanding Your Mandate

Your mandate acts as instructions for the automated investment system:
- Maintains target asset allocation
- Rebalances when portfolios drift
- Optimizes for tax efficiency
- Adapts to market conditions

## Digital Twin

### Overview
Your Digital Twin is an AI-powered simulation of your financial life. It uses your actual financial data to project future scenarios and test "what-if" situations without risking real money.

### Creating Your Profile

1. **Navigate to Digital Twin**
   - Features Hub â†’ Digital Twin
   - Click "Create Profile"

2. **Input Financial Data**
   - Current income and expenses
   - Assets and investments
   - Debts and liabilities
   - Risk tolerance
   - Life stage and goals

### Running Simulations

1. **Select Scenario Type**
   - Career change
   - Home purchase
   - Market crash
   - Retirement planning
   - Business venture

2. **Configure Parameters**
   - Time horizon (5-30 years)
   - Income changes
   - Major expenses
   - Investment returns
   - Inflation assumptions

3. **Run Monte Carlo Analysis**
   - System runs 100+ simulations
   - Accounts for randomness and volatility
   - Projects range of outcomes

### Interpreting Results

- **Median Outcome**: Most likely result
- **Best Case**: 90th percentile
- **Worst Case**: 10th percentile
- **Confidence Bands**: Range of likely outcomes
- **Risk Metrics**: Probability of success

### Using Insights
